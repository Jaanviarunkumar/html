🦸‍♀️ Superhero Squad 🦸‍♀️

🔥 Phoenixa
Real Name: Jaanvi
⚡ Powers:

Water power (primary), Invisibility, Super strength, Magic and spell casting, Time travel (forbidden, rarely used), Resurrection — rises when defeated!

😤 Weakness:

Anger — when Phoenixa gets angry, she causes destruction to the whole city!

💫 Personality:

Courageous, brave, and happy. Spreads happiness everywhere she goes. The leader of the squad!

📖 Description:

Phoenixa represents the legendary Phoenix bird. She protects the innocent, fights enemies, and always rises from defeat. She is the courageous leader of the superhero squad, spreading happiness everywhere she goes!

🐦 Quailina
Real Name: Madhivadhana
⚡ Powers:

Controls the wind, Super hearing, Lives in her own dreamy world!

😤 Weakness:

Gets scared easily — but always faces her fears and becomes stronger!

💫 Personality:

Sweet, kind, intelligent, and a little funny. Cares deeply for others. Usually lost in her own dreamy world!

📖 Description:

Quailina represents the quail bird. She may get scared easily, but she always faces her fears bravely and becomes stronger. She cares deeply for her friends and is always there when needed!

🦩 Flamingana
Real Name: Aarna (Phoenixa's sister!)
⚡ Powers:

Controls fire, Glitter magic, Creates destructive flames when emotional!

😤 Weakness:

Acts silly most of the time which makes others irritated. Sometimes gets really angry and makes everyone around her angry too!

💫 Personality:

Sweet, brave, somewhat kind, and funny. Sometimes gets angry and makes others around her feel the same!

📖 Description:

Flamingana represents the beautiful pink flamingo bird. She is Phoenixa's sister! She controls fire and glitter magic. When Phoenix gets angry, Flamingana helps cool things down — even though she can cause chaos herself with her fire and glitter powers!

🦅 Falconela
Real Name: Rithvika (Phoenixa's best friend!)
⚡ Powers:

Controls rocks, Can create earthquakes, Super courageous!

😤 Weakness:

Sitting alone for too long makes her sad and angry — when she gets angry, rocks surround her and she needs her friends to help her!

💫 Personality:

Kind, caring, a little funny, and sometimes overdramatic. Courageous but gets emotional when lonely!

📖 Description:

Falconela represents the mighty falcon bird. She is Phoenixa's best friend! She can control rocks and even create earthquakes. When she feels lonely and gets angry, rocks surround her — and only her friends can help her break free!
